About Us (Me):
This project started in October 2021.

I'm a native speaker of Portuguese, speak English, I'm studying Italian and I live in a country with three official languages, which means that eventually, I'll be learning the other languages.


I got to study a little German and every time I start studying a new language I struggle with the variations of the verb.

I really need repetition to assimilate the information and after a lot of searching for a site where I can simply choose a verb and practise all the variations, I decided to do it myself.

At the moment this text is being written, we have the option for Italian and the database already has more than 500 verbs, and growing.

English should be the second language, and then French, slowly and steadily.


I hope the site is as useful to you as it is to me. Your feedback is always welcome!
